about us

We are a team of game artists based in Toronto. With creative minds and high skills, we will take on any project, no matter the difficulty level. We will work with any game platform, from VR to desktop computers and mobile.

Our team is very flexible and each of us can work in different styles. Additionally, our team is fluent in 9 different languages including English, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish, Portuguese, Cantonese, and Vietnamese. So we can communicate with you in any of the listed languages that is most comfortable for you.

Well Played Games ready to develop a 2D or 3D concept for you or work by your concept. We appreciate your cooperation and are ready to pay maximum attention to your project.
